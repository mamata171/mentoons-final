Laughter therapy is a practice that harnesses the power of laughter to promote physical and emotional well-being, It involves intentional laughter to reduce stress, boost mood, and improve overall well-being. It's a fun and effective way to embrace the healing power of laughter.

Laughter therapy has been linked to reduced anxiety, improved immune function, and enhanced cardiovascular health. It complements conventional medical treatments and has shown promise in enhancing patients' quality of life, particularly in managing chronic pain and stress-related disorders.
