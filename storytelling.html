For 6-12 years old children we provide workshops on Social Human Development (Stories around thoughts, emotions, feelings, bonding, relationships, friendship, mindfulness, gratitude) and Behavioural Change (Respecting Elders)

Benifits of story telling

  • Engagement: Captures attention, holds interest, and maintains engagement with compelling narratives.
  • Memorability: Stories are memorable, aiding information retention and recall.
  • Connection: Evokes emotions, fosters empathy, and creates relatable connections between storytellers and audiences.
  • Simplification: Simplifies complex ideas, making them understandable through relatable examples.
  • Inspiration: Motivates action, instills resilience, and offers encouragement through impactful narratives.
